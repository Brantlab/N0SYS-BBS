We put the packet station on the air just before 1400 local with a 2-meter
mobile rig, a recycled TNC, and a laptop that had seen better decades.

## What worked

- A short, well-grounded feed line beat more transmit power.
- The old hardware TNC decoded weak frames more reliably than expected.
- A printed command card kept new operators moving.

The little station passed traffic all afternoon without touching the generator.
Proof, once again, that modest gear and patient operators can do useful work.
